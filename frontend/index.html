Common Questions

What is ATS and why does it matter? +
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) auto-rejects 75% of resumes before a human reads them. Without ATS optimization, you could be perfect for a role but never get a call.
How does Naukri daily profile update work? +
Naukri's algorithm shows recently active profiles first. We log in daily and refresh your profile, update key skills, and tweak your resume to keep you at the top of recruiter searches.
Do you apply to jobs on my behalf? +
Yes! In our Monthly and Power plans, we actively apply to relevant jobs on your behalf daily on Naukri and LinkedIn. You just attend the interviews.
